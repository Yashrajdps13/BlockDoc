Introducing BlockDoc, the cutting-edge app designed for healthcare professionals. Built on Flutter, our platform revolutionizes medical record-keeping by securely uploading patient files and reports onto the blockchain, ensuring unparalleled data integrity and confidentiality. 

With our integrated text summarizer model, doctors can quickly access concise summaries of patient records, saving valuable time and enhancing efficiency. Moreover, our advanced machine learning algorithms predict diagnoses for a range of diseases, including diabetes and breast cancer, empowering physicians with insightful insights for better patient care.

Backed by MongoDB, our robust database system ensures seamless storage and retrieval of patient information, guaranteeing reliability and scalability. Experience the future of healthcare management with MedChain - where innovation meets excellence.
